1. What is the Money App?

The money app was designed to help you take back control over your money. You can budget and track your spending on the app.

2. How much does it cost to use the Money App?

The Money App is a free app designed to help South Africans build wealth and break the poverty chains.

3. Where do I start using the Money App?

To get the most out of the Money App please follow the below:
a. Register and log in to the App
b. Set up your budget, starting with your income and then your expenses
c. Track your daily expenses using the Transactions window
d. Add and manage your accounts e.g Cheque account, credit account, Property etc.

4. How do I use the budget feature on the Money App?

Good question, Using the budget feature is very simple but you have to follow these steps:
a. Go to the budget window
b. Click the New Budget Item plus sign.
c. Select the income category. Put in the budgeted income first
c. Add expense items to your budget e.g Cellphone bill
d. assign a budget value to the items you just added. The app will not allow you to budget expenses that are more than the income

5. How do I track my transactions on the Money App?

You can capture your daily transactions on the Transactions window:
a. Make sure you have set up your budget first. You can not add transactions if your budget is not setup
b. Go to the Transactions window
c. Click the New Transactions plus sign
d select the category e.g. Personal Expenses
e. Select the Transaction Name e.g. Cellphone and input the transaction date and amount and click submit

6. How do I add a description to the transaction?

After capturing the transaction, you can click the edit icon "Pencil" and add the transaction description.

7. How do I edit the transaction amount

We encourage users not to update the amount. Rather delete the transaction and add a new one.

8. Why do I only see a few transaction names?

You only see the transaction names you have budgeted for. Please go to the Budget window and add items to your budget. Also, Budgeted items with zero budget will not be visible on the Transactions window.

9. How do I add a custom transaction name?

Click the config menu item and select Transaction Names Tab

10. What are accounts?

Accounts feature helps you keep track of your assets and liabilities. It also helps the App give you more personalised advice based on your overall financial picture.

11. How do I record negative values on Accounts?

Use '-' for negative values. e.g. -2500
